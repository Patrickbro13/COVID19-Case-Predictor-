About Predicto:

Predicto is a Machine Learning model which is trained with data from api.covid19.org.The data is validated by a group of volunteers and published into a Google sheet and an API.
Prediction of Number of COVID19 cases made by Predicto may vary with actual Number of COVID19 cases in India. Since this predictons are based on current trend of cases an unexpected event may create a huge change in trend
